Rocks and Grass

About:

To create a rock, start by removing the default Cube mesh. Now add an Ico Sphere mesh. You can begin by deforming the mesh a little if you would like. Now, change into edit mode by pressing tab. Scroll around your cube and press option to snap to an orthographic view. Hover over the Knife tool, then select Bisect. Click and drag across the mesh to create a cut. Open the menu for the cut and choose either Clear Inner or Clear Outer depending on what creates a better shape. Then, in the menu, click Fill to fill in the hole. Continue to scroll around the mesh while making cuts in the same way until you have a rock model that you are happy with. In order to create grass, start by removing the default Cube mesh. Now add a Cone mesh. Decrease the number of vertices of the mesh (it is best to set this to a small odd number). Use G to deform the shape of mesh and S to scale the mesh to a size of your liking. You have now created one blade of grass. Select the entirety of the mesh and press shift + D. This creates a duplicate of the blade of grass. Select the second blade of grass and again shape it using G and S. Continue this process until you have enough blades of grass to be satisfied with the resulting model.
